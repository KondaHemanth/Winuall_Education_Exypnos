Welcome! The following points give information about given google form questions and their meaning(context of the question):
(Kindly go through all the points and feel free to reach this page, in case you have any doubts while filling the form on the main web page.)

*Age: Individual's Age

*Gender: Individual's Gender

*Country: Individual's Country

*state: Individual's State

*breakup_loss: Did the Individual go through any breakup or loss in the past?

*family_history: Has there been any mental or physical health-related issues in the family?

*treatment: Has the individual gone through any mental or physical health-related treatments in the past or not?

*abuse: Has there been any record of abuse to the individual in the past?

*no_students: Number of students in the class.

*online_offline: Does the individual work online(outside the class) at least 50% of the time?

*coed_noncoed: Is the Institution of the individual coed or non-coed?
*food: Does the individual take care of his/her food habits?

*sleep: Does the individual get a sufficient amount of sleep on a regular basis?

*wellness_program: Has the individual been a part of any wellness program currently or in the past?

*seek_help: Does the individual ever tried or tries to seek help from his/her parents or teachers?

*anonymity: Does the individual try to remain anonymous(fearing it won't be protected) atleast 50% of the time?

*confidence_selfesteem: How is the confidence and self-esteem of the individual?

*mental_health_consequences: Has there been any visible consequences which you might think it's because of mental health issues.

*physical_health_consequences: Has there been any visible consequences which you might think it's because of mental health issues.

*classmates: Would the individual be willing to discuss a mental health issue with your classmates?

*counsellors: Would the individual be willing to discuss a mental health issue with your counsellors?

*mental_health_interview: Would the individual bring up a mental health issue he's been facing with the teacher/parent?

*physical_health_interview:Would the individual bring up a physical health issue he's been facing with the teacher/parent?

*mental_vs_physical: Does the individual's parents,teachers or classmates take mental health as seriously as physical health?
